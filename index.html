    About
    me

    Hi! I’m Rio Burhan, a multidisciplinary designer and web developer based in Indonesia. With extensive experience in design industry for years, I mainly focus on handling websites, landing pages, and create graphics for social media, promotion, and others.

    I started to delve into design as a way of my fondness for good visual and excitement of building creative ideas.

    My years of design experience come from designing various works for companies such as Media Indonesia, Parkson Retail Asia, Indosat, Unilever, Bank Mandiri, Gudang Garam, Toyota, and others.

    My Work Process

    This is my work process that applies commonly in any project.
    It’s easier to follow up and keeps the works stay on track.

    Collect

    Gathering all information that required in order to build the project such as images, texts, and other materials. Clear message is key to get best result.

    Design & Develop

    Starting the visual creation with layout and assets. In the website projects, the process will be continued with front-end and back-end development.

    Publish

    After the work is done, all final project files will be handed over to users. While in the web projects, the site will be put live with necessary optimization.
